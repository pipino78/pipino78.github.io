Psychotherapy and Counselling in Cork

“In my early professional years I was asking the question: How can I treat, or cure, or change this person? Now I would phrase the question in this way: How can I provide a relationship which this person may use for his own personal growth?”
― Carl R. Rogers

Group therapy provides a safe and therapeutic boundaries where people have the possibility to relate to others without being judged. Human beings are relational. We are all born through a relationship. The image of our Self comes into life by being with others. Group therapy is a great opportunity to explore how our limitations in relationships at times inhibit our authenticity. In our life we build structures, boundaries, develop habits which define our way of thinking. Group therapy is a space where people can revisit those structures and become aware of some of their limitations (suffering). It is a relational space where you can see yourself and see the other and have the opportunity to develop a genuine relationships within the group. Being in a true relationship can lead us to open up to the possibilities of who we can become…
