What is troubling you?

At various times in our life, issues can surface that make our journeys more difficult. These can be echoes from past experience that find voice or immediate challenges that disturb and distort our view of the world. Therapy can help you deal with difficult events and overcome challenging times in your life.

Group Therapy

Group therapy provides opportunities to explore interpersonal dynamics and develop insight into interpersonal patterns, how to respond to others, and how these responses impact the relationships. Group therapy gives members unique opportunities to explore their own interpersonal patterns within the group context, try out new ways of being with others in the safety of the group, and receive constructive interpersonal feedback from their fellow group members about how they are experienced and how they impact others.

My Approach

Is based on the integrative psychotherapy humanistic approach, which places the client and the psychotherapist on the same human level facilitating the therapeutic relationship and a deep acceptance of the Other as a Person. The work consists in creating a safe, confidential and supportive environment where the client will feel accepted and not judged. This approach draws from a number of rich theoretical frameworks such as Person Centred, Gestalt therapy, Transactional Analysis, Attachment, Complex Trauma, Character styles, IFS (Internal Family System) and Existential Therapy. I also make use of a Mindfulness approach which helps clients to be able to gain awareness of the present moment, reduces dwelling on the past and anxiety about the future.
